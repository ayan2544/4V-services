Who is 4V Services ?

4V Services is a software contracting company specializing in MarkLogic. Founded by Dave Cassel in 2018, the company has been working with clients and growing their team since then.

Dave Cassel worked for MarkLogic for almost 10 years, filling a variety of technical roles. He wrote The MarkLogic Cookbook and has blogged about MarkLogic since 2009.
